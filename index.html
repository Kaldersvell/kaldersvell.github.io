About Us

Kaldersvell OS is a Linux distribution founded on the principles of Open Source and all that is good in the world. We focus on providing a solid user experience for beginners and advanced users alike. Based off of Ubuntu and the GNOME 3 desktop, we are in the process of creating a Linux distro that we feel enhances a Linux user's experience. As if that isn't enough, Kaldersvell OS is free for all users!
